# Colour Pointclouds from Photographs
> As the scanner (VZ400i) can take photographs during the scans, you can colour your pointclouds based on these photographs, allowing nice visualisation.

1. Go to one-touch processing wizard. Toggle "Colorize Scans from Photos", use the settings to choose what photos you want to use.
2. Click Start
